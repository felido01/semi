EDUCATION

Lagos State University (LASU)

Degree: BSc in Electronics and Computer Engineering

Duration: 2023 - Expected Graduation 2027

The program majors in electronics/hardware and software engineering. It includes hands-on practical sessions and entails areas such as control systems, circuit design, and embedded systems. Additionally, core computer science topics such as Data Structures and Algorithms, Python programming, and Statistics are integral parts of the curriculum. LASU is a prestigious and well-known university in Nigeria, recognized for its excellence in engineering education.
